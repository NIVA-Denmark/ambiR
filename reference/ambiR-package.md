# ambiR: Calculate AZTI’s Marine Biotic Index

Calculate AZTI’s Marine Biotic Index \_AMBI\_, as well as the Shannon
Diversity Index \_H'\_ and the Danish benthic fauna quality index
\_DKI\_. \_Borja, A., J. Franco, V. Pérez, 2000. A marine biotic index
to establish the ecological quality of soft bottom benthos within
European estuarine and coastal environments Marine Pollution Bulletin
40(12) 1100-1114\_.
